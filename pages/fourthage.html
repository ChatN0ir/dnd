The Fourth Age:

Thus began the Fourth Age. The Age of Life. With the great portals of magic closed, and much of civilization destroyed, the races began to rebuild their towns and cities into a more functional, practical land. Hundreds of small villages and hamlets now provide food, textiles, and raw materials to the larger cities that have rebuilt themselves over the eons on Inis Skayle. The Capital city, Cathair Mor Gardenought (The City of Gardens) was built just outside the gates of the Great Crossing, where it is supposed the first Ogham Shrine was placed. Here both the gates and the Shrine are heavily protected and monitored by the King's Guard. Travel to and from the Westernlands is open, but heavily monitored, and while the trip is open to any honest creature, only daring traders, and others who stand to gain dare to cross it.
